# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Formal & Direct:** The copy should be clear, professional, and directly convey expertise.
- **Clarity Over Jargon:** Avoid unnecessary jargon, ensuring the text is understandable to both recruiters and technical peers.
- **Objective Tone:** Maintain an objective and confident tone when describing achievements and skills.

## Branding & Design System
- **Monochrome Minimalist:** Utilize a clean palette focused on black, white, and subtle grays to ensure maximum readability and a timeless professional look.
- **Typography:** Use clear, legible, and modern sans-serif fonts to support the minimalist design.
- **Whitespace:** Emphasize whitespace to create a structured and uncrowded visual hierarchy.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Performance First:** The website must load quickly with smooth transitions. Optimize assets and avoid heavy, unnecessary scripts.
- **Clear Navigation:** Structure the site so users can easily find projects, experience, and contact information with minimal clicks.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure high contrast and clear readability across all devices and screen sizes.
- **Mobile Responsiveness:** The minimalist design must translate seamlessly to mobile devices, maintaining navigation clarity and performance.
