# Project Definition

## Vision

To create a high-impact, professional, and minimalist personal website that serves as a digital resume and portfolio for Breno Battaglin. The site will showcase expertise, professional background, and contact information to recruiters, potential employers, and peers in the technical community.

## Target Audience

- **Recruiters/Employers:** Looking for detailed professional information and evidence of expertise.
- **Network/Peers:** Seeking professional contact and collaboration.
- **Technical Community:** Interested in technical skills and projects.

## Goals

- **Digital Resume:** Provide a comprehensive overview of skills, experience, and achievements.
- **Lead Generation/Contact:** Facilitate easy professional connection through clear contact options.

## Key Features

- **About Me Section:** Personal narrative, background, and professional journey.
- **Work/Projects Section:** Showcasing key projects and contributions.
- **Contact Information:** Direct links to professional profiles (Email, LinkedIn).

## Style & Vibe

- **Professional & Minimalist:** Clean, high-quality, and reliable design that focuses on content and clarity.
